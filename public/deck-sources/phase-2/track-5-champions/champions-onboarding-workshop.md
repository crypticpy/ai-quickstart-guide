# Deck Source: Track 5 Champions Onboarding Workshop

## How to use this file

Paste or upload this Markdown into an AI presentation tool and ask it to create a short facilitator support deck for champion onboarding. Do not expand it into a full training course. Localize champion role, tools, channels, and escalation contacts.

## Deck instructions

- Audience: AI Champions cohort
- Workshop length: 4 hours
- Desired deck length: 14 slides
- Tone: peer-oriented, practical, non-enforcement
- This is a facilitator support deck, not a full course deck
- Do not invent tool approvals, role authority, or HR/reporting obligations
