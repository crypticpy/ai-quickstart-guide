# Deck Source: Post-Launch And What Next Review

## How to use this file

Use this deck after the two-week stability watch to review stability, incidents, cost, user feedback, and readiness for the next project.

## Deck instructions

- Audience: sponsor, product owner, operations, Review Committee, technical lead
- Meeting length: 45-60 minutes
- Desired deck length: 10 slides
- Tone: operational, candid, improvement-oriented
- Use AI-generated images: optional; avoid visuals that imply scale approval before the review decision
- Do not invent usage metrics, incidents, savings, costs, or next-project readiness

## Localization checklist

- Replace all project, date, usage, incident, cost, readiness, owner, and decision placeholders.
- Confirm analytics, support, finance, eval, security, privacy, and operations data before presenting.
- Verify what can be shared with sponsors or oversight audiences.
- Apply agency branding only after content and evidence review.

## Import prompt

Create a post-launch review deck. Follow slide breaks exactly. Use speaker notes as presenter notes. Do not invent usage metrics, incidents, savings, costs, or readiness.
