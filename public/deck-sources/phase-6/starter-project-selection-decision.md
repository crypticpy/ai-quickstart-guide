# Deck Source: Starter Project Selection Decision

## How to use this file

Use this deck to help a sponsor group or Review Committee choose the first AI starter project. Replace candidates, scores, risk tiers, and recommendations with local data.

## Deck instructions

- Audience: sponsor group, AI Review Committee, product owners
- Meeting length: 45-60 minutes
- Desired deck length: 11 slides
- Tone: practical, comparative, decision-oriented
- Use AI-generated images: optional; avoid visuals that imply approval, surveillance, or invented authority
- Do not invent project readiness, ROI, risk scores, or approvals

## Localization checklist

- Replace all candidate, owner, score, risk tier, cost, and readiness placeholders.
- Confirm the shortlist came through the agency's intake and review process.
- Verify local privacy, records, procurement, labor, security, accessibility, and governance requirements.
- Apply agency branding only after content and evidence review.

## Import prompt

Create a starter-project decision deck. Follow slide breaks exactly. Use speaker notes as presenter notes. Do not invent readiness, ROI, risk scores, approvals, or agency facts.
