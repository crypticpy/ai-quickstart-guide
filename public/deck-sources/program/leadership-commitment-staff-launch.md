# Deck Source: Leadership Commitment And Staff Launch

## How to use this file

Use this deck to support a staff launch meeting or all-hands segment. Localize the signed commitment, approved tools status, and question channels before use.

## Deck instructions

- Audience: all staff
- Meeting length: 20-30 minutes
- Desired deck length: 9 slides
- Tone: transparent, practical, staff-centered
- Use AI-generated images: optional; avoid visuals that imply surveillance, job replacement, or invented authority
- Do not invent HR commitments, tool approvals, policy status, or agency facts

## Localization checklist

- Replace sponsor, program lead, dates, links, tool status, and contact placeholders.
- Confirm HR, labor, legal, privacy, records, security, accessibility, and communications review.
- Use only approved commitment language.
- Apply agency branding only after content review.
