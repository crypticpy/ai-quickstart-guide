# Deck Source: Legislative Or Oversight Briefing

## How to use this file

Use this for a council, legislative committee, oversight board, or public accountability briefing. Verify all law, budget, compliance, and performance claims with official sources and local counsel.

## Deck instructions

- Audience: legislative committee, council, oversight board, or public accountability body
- Meeting length: 10-20 minutes plus questions
- Desired deck length: 10 slides
- Tone: factual, transparent, non-technical, public-facing
- Use AI-generated images: optional; avoid visuals that imply legal approval, surveillance, or invented authority
- Do not invent laws, compliance status, metrics, savings, incidents, or approvals

## Localization checklist

- Replace all scope, compliance, budget, metric, incident, contact, and reporting placeholders.
- Verify laws, effective dates, compliance posture, and public-agency scope against official sources and local counsel.
- Confirm what can be disclosed publicly before presenting.
- Apply agency branding only after content and evidence review.

## Import prompt

Create an oversight briefing deck. Follow slide breaks exactly. Use speaker notes as presenter notes. Do not invent laws, compliance status, metrics, savings, incidents, citations, or approvals.
