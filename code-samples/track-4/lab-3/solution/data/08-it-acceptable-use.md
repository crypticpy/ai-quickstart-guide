# IT Acceptable Use

Department of Civic Operations
Policy DCO-IT-002, effective 2024-04-15

## Scope

This policy covers all Department-owned devices, accounts, and networks. It also covers personal devices when they are connected to Department resources.

## Authorized use

Department systems are for Department business. Limited personal use is allowed when it does not interfere with duties, does not consume significant resources, and does not violate any other policy.

## Account security

Staff use a unique account for each system. Passwords meet the current Department password standard, which requires at least sixteen characters or a passphrase of at least four words. Multi-factor authentication is required for all remote access and for any account with administrative privileges.

## Prohibited use

The following are prohibited.

- Sharing accounts or credentials.
- Disabling endpoint protection software.
- Connecting unmanaged devices to internal networks without prior approval.
- Installing software that is not on the approved software list, except where the staff member has documented administrative privileges and a documented business need.
- Accessing or storing material that is illegal, sexually explicit, or that promotes violence or harassment.

## Email

Department email is the only approved channel for sending Department information to external parties. Personal email accounts are not used for Department business. Auto-forwarding from a Department mailbox to a personal address is not permitted.

## Monitoring

Department systems are subject to monitoring for security and compliance purposes. Staff have no expectation of privacy in their use of Department systems.

## Reporting

Lost or stolen devices, suspected phishing, and any unusual system behavior are reported to the help desk within one hour of discovery.
