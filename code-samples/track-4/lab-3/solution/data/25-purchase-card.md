# Purchase Card Program

Department of Civic Operations
Policy DCO-FIN-016, effective 2024-03-15

## Eligibility

A Department purchase card is issued to a permanent staff member at the request of the division director. The cardholder completes the purchase card training before the card is activated.

## Limits

Default limits are $5,000 per single transaction and $10,000 per monthly cycle. Higher limits require written justification and approval from the Department finance director. Limits are reviewed annually.

## Allowed uses

The card is for Department business expenses up to the micro-purchase threshold and for travel expenses pre-authorized in the travel system. Allowed expenses include office supplies, conference registrations, and routine maintenance items.

## Prohibited uses

The following are prohibited on the purchase card.

- Personal expenses, including any incidental personal item.
- Cash advances.
- Splitting a purchase across multiple transactions to stay under a limit.
- Gift cards, except where pre-approved for an authorized program.
- Alcohol, except where allowed by a specific approved event budget.

## Receipts and reconciliation

The cardholder uploads a receipt for every transaction within five business days. The supervisor reviews and approves the monthly statement within ten business days of cycle close. Disputed transactions are reported to the card issuer within sixty days.

## Lost or stolen cards

Lost or stolen cards are reported to the card issuer and to the Department finance office within twenty-four hours of discovery.

## Misuse

Misuse of the purchase card is grounds for discipline up to and including termination and may be referred to the Inspector General for investigation. Repayment of any improper charge is required.
