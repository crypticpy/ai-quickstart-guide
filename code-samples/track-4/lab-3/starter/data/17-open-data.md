# Open Data

Department of Civic Operations
Policy DCO-IT-027, effective 2024-11-01

## Default open

Department datasets are open by default. A dataset is closed only when a specific legal restriction applies, when publication would harm an individual, or when the data quality is too low to publish responsibly.

## Inventory

Each division maintains an inventory of its datasets in the Department data catalog. The inventory captures dataset name, description, owner, update cadence, format, and classification.

## Publication

Open datasets are published on the public open data portal in non-proprietary, machine-readable formats. Each dataset has a published schema, a data dictionary, and a contact for questions. Update cadence is documented and met.

## Quality

Before first publication, a dataset goes through a quality review covering accuracy, completeness, timeliness, and privacy. The data owner approves publication. Significant changes to schema or methodology are announced on the dataset page and through the open data mailing list.

## Licensing

Open datasets are published under a permissive license, by default Creative Commons Attribution. Datasets that contain content under another license carry a notice on the dataset page.

## Retirement

A dataset that is no longer maintained is marked retired on the portal with a clear notice and remains available for at least one year before removal, unless removal is required by law.

## Feedback

Users may file feedback through the open data portal. Feedback is routed to the data owner and resolved within thirty business days where reasonable.
