# Vendor Onboarding

Department of Civic Operations
Policy DCO-FIN-014, effective 2024-05-01

## Registration

A vendor must complete registration in the Department vendor portal before any purchase order is issued. Registration captures legal name, doing-business-as name, taxpayer identification number, primary contact, banking information for direct deposit, and certifications.

## Required certifications

Vendors certify the following.

- The business is in good standing with the Secretary of State.
- The business is not on the federal excluded parties list.
- The business has not been debarred or suspended by any government within the last seven years, or has disclosed any such action.
- The business complies with applicable equal employment opportunity laws.
- For purchases above $50,000, the business has filed the most recent year's required tax returns.

False certifications are grounds for cancellation of any award and may be referred to the Inspector General.

## Tax compliance check

Before a first payment, the Finance division verifies tax compliance through the state tax compliance system. A vendor with an unresolved liability is held in pending status until the liability is resolved or a payment plan is in place.

## Insurance

Vendors providing services on Department property carry general liability insurance with a minimum limit of $1,000,000 per occurrence and $2,000,000 aggregate. Vendors providing professional services carry professional liability insurance with a minimum limit of $1,000,000 per claim. Certificates of insurance are uploaded to the vendor portal and renewed annually.

## Diversity certifications

Diversity certifications, including small business, woman-owned business, and minority-owned business, are recorded in the vendor portal where the vendor holds them. The Department reports diversity spend annually to the Office of the Comptroller.

## Maintenance

Vendors update their record within thirty days of any material change. The Department audits a sample of vendor records annually and corrects discrepancies.
