# Permits and Licensing

Department of Civic Operations
Policy DCO-OPS-011, effective 2024-07-15

## Application

Permits and licenses are requested through the Department permitting portal. The application captures applicant identity, scope, location, and any required attachments. Paper applications are accepted at the Department service counter and are entered into the portal by counter staff.

## Fees

Standard fee schedule is published on the Department website and updated each fiscal year. Fees are non-refundable once an application is accepted for review. A reduced fee is available for low-income applicants who qualify under the published criteria.

## Review timelines

Routine permits are reviewed within ten business days of a complete application. Permits that require external review, including health, fire, or zoning, are reviewed within thirty business days. The Department posts current actual review times on the public dashboard each month.

## Conditions and inspections

Issued permits may include conditions, including required inspections at named milestones. Inspections are scheduled through the portal. A failed inspection includes a written list of items to be corrected and a re-inspection date.

## Renewals

Renewals are sent to the applicant at least sixty days before the expiration date. A late renewal incurs a late fee equal to twenty-five percent of the renewal fee.

## Denials and appeals

Denials are issued in writing with the reason and a citation to the rule that supports the decision. Applicants may appeal a denial to the Department permits review board within thirty days of the denial.

## Public posting

Issued permits are posted to the public permits dashboard within five business days of issuance, with personally identifiable information of individual applicants redacted as required.
