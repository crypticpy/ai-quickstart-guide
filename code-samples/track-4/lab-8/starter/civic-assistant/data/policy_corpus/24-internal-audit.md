# Internal Audit

Department of Civic Operations
Policy DCO-AUD-001, effective 2023-04-01

## Independence

The Department internal audit function reports administratively to the Department head and functionally to the Audit Committee. Auditors do not have operational responsibility for the activities they audit.

## Annual plan

The internal audit team prepares a risk-based annual audit plan. The plan considers financial materiality, operational risk, and compliance risk. The plan is reviewed by the Audit Committee at the start of the fiscal year and adjusted as risks change.

## Engagement process

Each engagement starts with a written notification, a kickoff meeting, and a written scope. Fieldwork includes document review, interviews, and testing. Findings are discussed with management before the report is drafted. Management responses are included in the final report.

## Reports

Final reports go to the unit being audited, the responsible division director, the Department head, and the Audit Committee. Reports are summarized in the public annual audit report.

## Follow-up

Each finding has a target completion date. The audit team tracks open findings and reports status to the Audit Committee quarterly. Findings open more than one past their target date are escalated to the Department head.

## External audits

The Department cooperates fully with the State Auditor and with grantor audits. The internal audit team coordinates audit responses to reduce duplication.

## Records

Audit work papers are retained for ten years.
