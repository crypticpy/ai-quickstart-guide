The intake form captures one idea. The inventory is the register that survives the review meeting. It records what the agency knows about each AI use case: who owns it, which department it affects, its current tier, its status, and when it needs review again.

This page provides a lightweight browser-only inventory for agencies that do not yet have a portfolio tool. It is intentionally simple: add reviewed ideas, export CSV or Markdown, and move the export into the agency system of record.

## Minimum fields

| Field | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Use case | Human-readable name used in meeting minutes and reports |
| Sponsor | Person accountable for the workflow and follow-up |
| Department | Where the idea originated and who will pilot it |
| Tier | Current risk classification from the Review Committee |
| Status | Submitted, approved, deferred, retired, or needs refinement |
| Next review | Date the committee rechecks status, controls, or law changes |

## Operating rule

The inventory is updated only after committee review. Staff should submit ideas through the [AI Use Case Intake Form](/phase-2-education/use-case-intake/); the program lead then copies approved or deferred ideas into the inventory after the tier is confirmed.
