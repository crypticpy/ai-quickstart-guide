Use these Markdown files when you need a presentation faster than building slides from scratch. They are designed for AI presentation generation tools and agent workflows. Paste or upload the Markdown into your preferred tool, ask it to follow the slide breaks, then localize, verify, brand, and review the output before presenting.

These are **not finished decks**. They are source material for a first draft. Replace every placeholder, verify every claim, and route legal, procurement, labor, accessibility, and communications-sensitive material through your agency's normal review path.

## How to use a deck source

1. Open the deck source.
2. Paste or upload it into Gamma, Beautiful.ai, ChatGPT, Claude, PowerPoint Copilot, or another presentation workflow.
3. Use the prompt in the file's "Deck instructions" section.
4. Preserve the slide breaks unless you intentionally want the tool to restructure the deck.
5. Replace local placeholders such as `[Agency]`, `[program lead]`, `[approved tool]`, and `[date]`.
6. Verify policy, legal, budget, staffing, and source claims before presenting.
7. Apply agency branding and accessibility review last.
