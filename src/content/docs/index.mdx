## What this is

A complete, sequenced 12-month roadmap that any government agency can follow — from "we want to do AI" to "we have a modular platform running our first production AI application." The guide is open source, stack-agnostic, and includes the governance, education, and infrastructure required to sustain AI work past the first pilot.

It is built from Austin Public Health's real-world experience and perspective.

## Six phases over 12 months

  </Card>

## Pick your path

Different agency sizes need different timelines and depth. Choose one — your selection is saved in your browser and shown in the **Path** dropdown at the top of every page, and the per-phase pages will surface the variant that matches.

<AgencyPathPicker client:load />

See the [Pick Your Path](/getting-started/pick-your-path/) page for the full variant comparison and the month-by-month Small-Agency walkthrough.

> **Off-ramps are real.** Agencies can stop after Phase 1 (governance only), Phase 2 (governance + education), or Phase 5 (platform without starter project) and still have a complete, valuable deliverable. The roadmap is designed so partial completion still beats no completion.

## How to use this site

- **Working through the guide:** start with the [Readiness Assessment](/getting-started/readiness-assessment/), then follow the [90-Day Quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart-checklist/).
- **Just looking up a template:** every interactive template (AUP wizard, risk-tier matrix, ROI calculator) lives inside its phase and exports to your local machine — nothing is sent anywhere.
- **Deploying your own copy:** click "Use this template" on the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/crypticpy/ai-quickstart-guide) → your agency owns its copy, online or offline.
- **Downloading the PDF:** every release ships a combined PDF and DOCX. See the latest on the [Releases page](https://github.com/crypticpy/ai-quickstart-guide/releases).
