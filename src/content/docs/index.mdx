## What this is

A complete, sequenced 12-month roadmap that any government agency can follow, from "we want to do AI" to "we have a modular platform running our first production AI application." The guide is open source, stack-agnostic, and includes the governance, education, and infrastructure required to sustain AI work past the first pilot.

It is built from working government agencies' delivery experience.

Start here if you are:

- **Trying to get oriented:** run the readiness assessment, then choose a path and role below.
- **Looking for a specific artifact:** use the phase navigation or template links; interactive tools export locally.
- **Adapting this for your agency:** treat the recommendations as a starting system, then localize policy, procurement, staffing, and legal review.

See the [Pick Your Path](/getting-started/pick-your-path/) page for the full variant comparison and the month-by-month Small-Agency walkthrough. Your selected path is saved in your browser. On desktop, it appears in the **Path** dropdown at the top of every page; on phones, reopen this guided route or the Pick Your Path page to adjust it.

> **Off-ramps are real.** Agencies can stop after Phase 1 (governance only), Phase 2 (governance + education), or Phase 5 (platform without starter project) and still have a complete, valuable deliverable. The roadmap is designed so partial completion still beats no completion.

## How to use this site

- **Working through the guide:** [run the readiness assessment](/getting-started/readiness-assessment/), then [follow the 90-day quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart-checklist/).
- **Just looking up a template:** every interactive template (AUP wizard, risk-tier matrix, ROI calculator) lives inside its phase and exports to your local machine; nothing is sent anywhere.
- **Deploying your own copy:** [open the GitHub repo](https://github.com/crypticpy/ai-quickstart-guide), click "Use this template," and your agency owns its copy, online or offline.
- **Downloading the PDF:** every release ships a combined PDF and DOCX. [Download the latest release](https://github.com/crypticpy/ai-quickstart-guide/releases).
